List of All Native Leica M Mount Cameras

Organizing all native Leica M Mount cameras

Aug 16, 2025
6 minute read

Since Leica M3 was announced in 1954, Leica M mount was officially born, and has gone through seventy years of history so far. This mount not only carries Leica’s own classic M series rangefinder cameras, but also attracted many other brands (Minolta, Zeiss, Konica, Voigtländer, Rollei, Epson, Ricoh, Pixii etc.) to successively launch compatible models, forming a huge M mount ecosystem.

Many enthusiasts must be curious: “What are all the M mount cameras?” The following table organizes the complete list of M mount cameras from Leica and major brands, from 1954 Leica M3 to 2024 latest Pixii Max.
