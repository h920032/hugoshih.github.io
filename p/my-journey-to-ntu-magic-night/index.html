Recording my thoughts on participating in NTU Magic Night over the years

13 minute read

My Journey to NTU Magic Night

Preface

Three years later, sitting in the audience again to appreciate NTU Magic Night. Different from the nervousness when I was a stage crew, a performer, or a core cadre watching Magic Night. I was once again moved by this magical evening. Recalling when I first stepped into the club class in my freshman year, watching Ding Hao perform circus tricks. In a blink of an eye, I am about to put on the graduation scarf. For four years, the Magic Club has been home, the center of life, and an inseparable part. So let’s summarize it with this reflection article.

The Road to Magic Night

Origin

The story began in high school when a senior from the Magic Club showed me a video of Hua Nei performing Le plus. The video played, and he talked endlessly: “In the distant NTU Magic Club, there is a group of very powerful magicians, and no one knows why they are so powerful.” This made me very curious. Later I learned that these people had been on the NTU Magic Night. What kind of stage is that? Is it really as powerful as the rumors?

19th Magic Night “The League of Performers”

The opportunity came quickly. In the winter vacation Magic is Concept event, I accidentally won two tickets for the 19th Magic Night. Then I lobbied Yong Chen and friends from the club to go watch together. In that environment where we could only watch Magic Talent Show to appreciate magic, NTU Magic Night showed me a new world of magic. Nine incredibly powerful performances and hosting that made people laugh from beginning to end. This is really amazing. I must stand on this stage one day.

20th Magic Night “Lumos”

In the second semester of third year high school, even though I became a college entrance exam warrior, I still got a ticket for the 20th Magic Night. Unfortunately, only half of the show was finished by 9:30, so I could only give up the idea of finishing Magic Night. Later I heard about various wonderful things in the second half, and I really regretted it. If I had known that the next time I could sit in the audience would be four years later, I would probably regret it even more QQ.

21st Magic Night “21Hero”

Then I muddle-headedly entered NTU. Muddle-headedly joined the NTU Magic Club. Muddle-headedly watched Yun Heng perform magic (Sorry Yun Heng QAQ, I often disappeared from club classes…) Unknowingly, the time left for magic became less and less. Until one day I really believed that my passion for magic was probably just this much. Then Yong Chen suddenly told me he was asked to be a stage crew for Magic Night. He saw a lot of amazing things there. Out of curiosity, I later also applied to be a stage crew and was lucky enough to be selected. Stepping into the sacred place of the 2nd Activity Center, I saw many incredible things. What shocked me first was Yang Yi’s incredibly powerful performance. Followed by each performer, under the meticulous carving of the elders day and night, Evolving and transforming rapidly in a very short time, becoming amazingly cool performances. It made me believe again that becoming a performer for NTU Magic Night is not an unattainable dream.

22nd Magic Night “Fantastical Beasts and Where to Find Them”

Driven by the Magic Night syndrome, I took the teaching exam and accepted the baptism of being a cadre for a whole year. It can be considered as making up for the things I didn’t learn well when I was a freshman. Then hurriedly completed the selection process. After passing the selection, I wasted the time before the first verification, and started various Magic Night journeys of rushing progress. After the breakdown of the fifth verification, I finally completed a routine that I was reasonably satisfied with. Finally stood on the stage of NTU Magic Night, fulfilling the promise I made to myself in high school. There are too many people to thank along the way. 23rd Magic Night “Secret”

Magic Night syndrome struck again, and I took over as a core cadre. Very grateful to the previous core cadres for believing that I was competent for this job. Being a core cadre was where I learned the most things in the Magic Club. Including various administrative affairs, crisis management. How to organize an event well, how to plan a good evening party. How to lead a group of performers, from knowing nothing to finally standing on the stage of NTU Magic Night. And the process is still to thank the help of many NTU elders.

24th Magic Night “Magician and the ideal land”

Unloaded the identity of a core cadre. Have more time to try things I want to try. Three years later, sitting in the audience again to appreciate NTU Magic Night. Returned to the excited and expectant mood when first learning magic. And the emotion after watching Magic Night lingered for a long time. Sure enough, only NTU Magic Night can surpass NTU Magic Night. NTU Magic Night is like a very long dream. After waking up from it, You will know it is time to face your life properly. Thank you to the core cadres of this session. Thank you Fu Miao, Shi Fu, Aaron, Yan Yu. Thank you for doing your best to support the club. Thank you to the performers of this session. Thank you for surviving various verifications and tests. Various hard work will turn into sweet fruits. Hope you all enjoyed this stage full of fantasy and charm.
